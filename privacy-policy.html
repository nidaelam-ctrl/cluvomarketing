Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cluvo Marketing ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit www.cluvo.ca or contact us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you fill out our contact form:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company name and size
  • Any additional information you provide in the message field

We also collect non-personal information automatically, such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site, through standard web analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and schedule strategy calls
  • Send you relevant information about our services (only if you've opted in)
  • Improve our website and services

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are transmitted securely via Formsubmit.co and delivered to our team email. We retain your contact details only for as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry or as required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Formsubmit.co — processes contact form submissions
  • GitHub Pages — hosts our website

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@cluvo.ca.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@cluvo.ca.
